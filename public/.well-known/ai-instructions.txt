# ReloFinder.ch

> Compare Swiss relocation agencies, read verified reviews, and get free quotes. The independent platform helping expats find the right relocation support in Switzerland.

## About
ReloFinder is Switzerland's independent relocation agency comparison platform. We help expats and HR managers find, compare, and connect with verified relocation companies across all Swiss cantons.
