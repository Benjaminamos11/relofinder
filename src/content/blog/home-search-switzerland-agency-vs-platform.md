## 2. The Anatomy of a Winning Rental Dossier

In Switzerland, your dossier is your resume. If it isn't flawless, you won't even be invited to a viewing.

### The Essential Checklist for 2026:
- **Betreibungsauszug**: Your debt collection registry extract (must be under 3 months old).
- **Proof of Solvency**: A signed Swiss work contract or a bank statement showing several months' rent coverage.
- **Reference Letters**: A personal "recommendation" from a local professional or a preceding landlord.
- **The Application Letter**: A formal, German-translated letter (even in Zurich) explaining why you are the ideal tenant for this specific building.
