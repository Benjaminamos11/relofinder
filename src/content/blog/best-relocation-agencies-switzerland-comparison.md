## How to Compare Quotes

When you receive a quote via ReloFinder, look for these three hidden costs:

### 1. "Third-Party Fees"

Does the quote include the permit fees, or just the *service* of applying?

**Example:** Work permit application = CHF 150-400 (government fee). Some agencies quote their "service fee" of CHF 800 *plus* the government fee. Others include it. Always clarify.

### 2. Home Search Limits

Is it "unlimited viewings" or "up to 5 viewings"?

**Reality Check:** In Zürich's 0.07% vacancy market, 5 viewings is rarely enough. Ask what happens when you exceed the limit — is it CHF 200 per additional viewing? CHF 500? Unlimited?

### 3. Departure Services

Is the handover protocol included when you leave?

**What to Check:**
- Lease termination assistance
- Final inspection coordination
- Deposit recovery support
- Utility cancellation

Many "arrival" packages exclude departure entirely. You'll pay again in 2-3 years.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much do relocation agencies charge in Switzerland?

Full-service relocation packages in Switzerland typically range from CHF 6,000 to CHF 12,000. Corporate "white glove" agencies charge CHF 10,000-15,000+. Boutique agencies offer selective services for CHF 4,000-8,000. DIY approaches using digital tools like [Expat-Services.ch](https://expat-services.ch) can reduce costs to CHF 500-2,000.

### What's the difference between corporate and boutique relocation agencies?

**Corporate agencies (Tier 1)** are global firms with offices in 50+ countries, standardized processes, and in-house immigration teams. Best for large company moves where HR pays directly.

**Boutique agencies (Tier 2)** are Swiss-owned, owner-operated firms offering personalized "high-touch" service. They know local landlords personally and provide direct consultant access. Ideal for executives and families who want flexibility.

### Can I handle relocation myself without an agency?

Yes. The "Digital DIY" approach (Tier 3) works well for tech-savvy professionals relocating within Europe. Platforms like [Expat-Services.ch](https://expat-services.ch) offer AI tools for document generation and translation, verified directories of service providers, and glossaries to navigate Swiss bureaucracy independently.

### What hidden fees should I watch for in relocation quotes?

Three key areas:
1. **Third-party fees:** Does the quote include government permit costs, or just the service fee?
2. **Home search limits:** "Unlimited viewings" vs. "up to 5 viewings" — what happens when you exceed the limit?
3. **Departure services:** Is lease termination, final inspection, and deposit recovery included?
