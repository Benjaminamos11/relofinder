## Section 1: The "Silent" Market Explained

Why would a landlord *not* want to list their property on the biggest portals? The answer is simple: **Administrative Overload.**

A single attractive listing in Seefeld or Zug’s old town can generate over 500 emails in a single afternoon. For many private landlords and boutique property managers, this is a nightmare. Instead, they prefer "silent rentals." They send their available inventory directly to trusted **[relocation agencies](/companies)** or wait for a **Swiss Nachmieter without agency**—a departing tenant who finds their own replacement.
