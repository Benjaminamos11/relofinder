Langpartner offers highly personalized, traditional brokerage services. They are particularly favored by corporate executives who prefer a classical advisory relationship. ## #4. Helvebroker SA
For expats settling in the French-speaking Romandie region (Geneva, Vaud, Neuchâtel) or navigating cross-border worker (Frontalier) complexities, Helvebroker SA is an excellent choice. They specialize in the unique tax and insurance treaties between Switzerland and France, providing critical guidance for workers commuting across borders. They are competent and professional, serving their niche effectively.

## #5. VZ VermögensZentrum
While technically a bank and wealth management firm rather than a pure broker, VZ VermögensZentrum deserves a spot on this list for expats nearing retirement. They offer extremely sophisticated pension planning and tax optimization strategies. However, their services are generally tailored for High-Net-Worth individuals with substantial assets already in the Swiss system, making them less accessible for standard expats just arriving in the country.
